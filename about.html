About Me

Designer, Researcher, and Creative Problem Solver

My Journey

My journey into UX design began in the mountains of Guizhou and led me through Pennsylvania, Seattle, and now Ann Arbor. This diverse path has shaped my approach, blending technology, cultural understanding, and creativity.

With over three years of experience in the field, I earned my B.D. in Design from the University of Washington in 2021 and transitioned from a design intern to a UX Designer at iFlytek, where I focused on creating innovative B2B EdTech experiences.

After that, I pursued an M.S. from the University of Michigan to further hone my skills as a designer and researcher, committed to creating meaningful digital solutions that bridge technology and human needs.
